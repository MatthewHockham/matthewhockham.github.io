About Hot Beans Web

Hot Beans Web is an upcoming company founded in 2024 aiming to create amazing websites for small businesses. We currently have 4 members of the team and are looking to grow to increase our work flow and extend our help to all our clients.

At the moment, we are partnered with 5 companies which we run their domain and website.

Our Team

Our team is made up of friendly, skilled people who are passionate about helping small businesses succeed online. From building websites to making them work smoothly and look great, everyone here plays an important role in bringing your ideas to life. Get to know the people behind Hot Beans Web!

Bob Speight

CEO/Founder

He knows how important a good website is for a small business to show off what they do alongside keep financies, positive. Job Section

Javascript Programmer

We’re looking for a skilled JavaScript Programmer to join the Hot Beans Web team. In this role, you'll be responsible for building and maintaining high-quality JavaScript frameworks and tools. You'll collaborate with designers, backend developers, and the CEO to create seamless user experiences for our clients.

What you'll be doing:

  • Building and maintaining web apps using JavaScript (think React, Vue, etc.)
  • Collaborating with the team on new ideas and features
  • Making things fast, smooth, and user-friendly

What we're looking for:

  • Solid JavaScript skills
  • Experience with at least one modern framework (React, Vue, .etc)
  • Comfort with HTML/CSS and basic responsive design
  • A good understanding of Git and how to work in a team

Why join us?

  • Real impact from day one
  • Flexible hours and remote-friendly
  • We’re growing — and you’ll grow with us

Website Designer

We’re looking for a creative and detail-oriented Website Designer to join our team. In this role, you'll be responsible for designing clean, engaging, and user-friendly websites that align with our clients goals as a small business. You'll collaborate closely with developers, javascript developers, and other team members to bring promotion for a company to life.

What you'll be doing:

  • Designing beautiful, user-friendly websites (mostly for web, sometimes mobile)
  • Turning concepts into wireframes, mockups, and finished layouts
  • Collaborating with developers to make sure your designs actually work
  • Helping define and maintain our design style and visual identity

What we're looking for:

  • Solid experience with web design tools (Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch — your choice)
  • A portfolio that shows off your style and skills
  • Understanding of basic UX principles and responsive design
  • A creative mindset and a willingness to try new things

Why join us?

  • Freedom to experiment and take creative risks
  • Flexible hours and remote-friendly
  • We’re growing — and you’ll grow with us
